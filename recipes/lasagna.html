Lasagna

Description

Good day moms! this website is going to provide a step by step super easy process on how to make a tasty and healthy lasagna

Well time to cook!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
